Betterway's toilet paper is a direct-to-consumer alternative to conventional tissue, designed to be soft, strong, and suitable for everyday use. Made from bamboo rather than traditional tree-based pulp, the rolls are packaged without plastic and shipped in compact cardboard boxes. The product is positioned as a simple household staple for consumers looking to reduce their environmental footprint without sacrificing comfort or performance.

Sustainability spotlight

Betterway highlights the use of bamboo, a fast-growing, renewable resource that regenerates without replanting and requires less water than conventional trees. The toilet paper is marketed as plastic-free, tree-free, and biodegradable, helping reduce deforestation and single-use plastic waste. While these material choices represent a clear environmental improvement over standard toilet paper, the brand provides limited detail on manufacturing locations, chemical processing methods, and third-party certifications, which makes it harder to independently verify the full lifecycle and labor impacts.
