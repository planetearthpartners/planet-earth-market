The Koala Sofa Bed is a compact, modern sleeper sofa that effortlessly transforms from a sleek loveseat into a firm, full- or queen-sized foam bed, with no metal bars, all in seconds. Exceptionally easy to assemble thanks to its tool-free design and lightweight flat-pack packaging, it's ideal for small spaces and occasional overnight guests. Reviewers praise its clean aesthetic, sturdy build, and surprisingly comfortable sleep surface for less-than-regular use, though it leans on the firmer side, it excels for short-term sleeping scenarios.

Sustainability spotlight

Crafted with a frame made from 100% FSC-certified timber, Koala side-steps deforestation and supports responsible forestry practices. The cushions feature CertiPUR-US® certified foam, free of heavy metals, ozone depleters, formaldehyde, and other harmful chemicals, and the luxe fabric includes up to 30% recycled polyester for reduced environmental impact. As a certified B Corporation and member of 1% for the Planet, Koala invests in habitat restoration, carbon-neutral operations, and sustainable product design that gives back to communities and ecosystems.
