Grove Collaborative is an online marketplace offering home, personal care, wellness, and household essentials with a focus on plant-based ingredients, reduced-waste packaging, and safer everyday products. The platform includes both Grove's private-label products and a curated selection of third-party brands that meet Grove's ingredient and environmental standards. Designed for repeat household use, Grove supports subscription and on-demand ordering while emphasizing reusability, ingredient transparency, and lower-toxicity alternatives to conventional home goods.

Sustainability spotlight

Grove Collaborative demonstrates a broad commitment to climate accountability, plastic reduction, and responsible sourcing across both its marketplace and private-label offerings. As a company, Grove is a Certified B-Corp, Climate Neutral® Certified, and plastic neutral, offsetting operational emissions and collecting an equivalent amount of plastic to what it sells each year. Grove ships orders using plastic-free packaging whenever possible and publishes clear ingredient standards that restrict harsh chemicals, synthetic fragrances, and unnecessary additives across approved brands.

Grove's private-label products represent the strongest sustainability performance on the platform, featuring plant-based formulations, concentrated refills, and reusable glass or aluminum packaging designed to reduce single-use plastic waste over time. Third-party brands sold on Grove vary in sustainability depth, but are generally aligned around clean ingredients, ethical sourcing, and recyclable or refill-friendly packaging. While not every product is fully circular or zero-waste, Grove's transparency, refill systems, and verified certifications place it among the more credible and accessible sustainable home goods marketplaces.
