The Outerknown Blanket Shirt is an overshirt-style classic made from lofty, brushed 100% organic cotton twill that strikes the perfect balance of soft yet substantial, ideal for layering through three seasons. With a relaxed, versatile fit and details like chest flap pockets and corozo nut buttons (a plant-based alternative to plastic), it's both functional and timeless. Offered in more than twenty rich plaid or garment-dyed solid colorways, it's frequently called "the coziest shirt ever made" by fans and reviewers alike.

Sustainability spotlight

Outerknown is deeply committed to ethical and eco-conscious production, its Blanket Shirt is crafted using organic cotton (eliminating synthetic pesticides and reducing environmental harm) and corozo nut buttons harvested from tagua palm nuts on the forest floor. The brand also champions regenerative organic cotton programs (including financial support and guaranteed purchasing for farmers in Peru), full supply-chain transparency, and a long-term goal of achieving 100% circularity by 2030.
