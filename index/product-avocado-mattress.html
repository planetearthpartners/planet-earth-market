Avocado's flagship Green Mattress is a premium latex-hybrid bed handcrafted in Los Angeles using GOTS-certified organic cotton, organic wool, and GOLS-certified organic latex layered atop recycled-steel pocketed coils arranged in seven ergonomic zones for exceptional support, edge stability, and breathability. Available in Firm, Medium, and Plush Pillow-Top or Box-Top models (the latter adding FSC-certified Talalay latex), it adapts gracefully to back, side, stomach, and combo sleepers while offering superior temperature regulation and motion isolation. Backed by a 365-night sleep trial and a 25-year limited warranty, it delivers lasting comfort and peace of mind.

Sustainability spotlight

Avocado is a Climate Neutral® and Fair Trade® certified B-Corp deeply committed to transparency and environmental stewardship. Its mattresses meet high-bar certifications, including OEKO-TEX Standard 100, MADE SAFE®, GREENGUARD Gold, and 1% for the Planet, and are made from biodegradable or recycled materials, with efforts to divert more than 80% of manufacturing waste from landfills and offset carbon emissions as part of its "Dream of Better" initiative.
