Saatva's Saatva Classic Mattress is a luxury innerspring-hybrid bed made in the U.S., featuring an organic cotton cover, comfort foams, and a dual-layer system of recycled steel coils designed to enhance support, airflow, and durability. Offered in Plush Soft, Luxury Firm, and Firm options, it accommodates a wide range of sleep positions while providing strong edge support and responsiveness. Unlike most online mattresses, Saatva delivers beds uncompressed via in-home white-glove service. The mattress is backed by a 365-night sleep trial and a lifetime warranty, emphasizing long-term use over fast replacement.

Sustainability spotlight

Saatva focuses on lower-impact materials and product longevity, using organic cotton, natural latex in select models, and CertiPUR-US® certified foams made without ozone depleters, heavy metals, or formaldehyde. The brand avoids fiberglass flame retardants and reduces single-use plastic waste by shipping mattresses full-size instead of vacuum-sealed. While Saatva demonstrates progress through durable construction and health-conscious materials, it is not Climate Neutral®, not a B-Corp, and does not disclose a full carbon footprint or life-cycle assessment, limiting transparency compared to leading eco-mattress brands.
