About Planet Earth Market

Planet Earth Market is a curated marketplace for sustainable-living brands: personal care, cleaning, apparel, sleep, kitchen, and grocery. Every product in the marketplace is scored against the same sustainability and impact criteria, and most are also personally purchased and used by our small editorial team before we write a word about them.

We don't hold inventory or run checkout ourselves. When you click "Check Price," you're taken directly to the brand's own site to complete your purchase, some of those links are affiliate links, which we disclose clearly on every page (see our Affiliate Disclosure).

Each product carries an impact scorecard across three categories, Environmental, Supply Chain, and Social, built from the certifications and practices each brand discloses publicly. Read more about how that scoring works on our Vetting Standards page.
